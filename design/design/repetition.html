Repetition

Company Name

Fox News

How Used

Repetition - Fox news does a great job with repetition on their webpages within their website. As I went through several of their different pages I noticed that the headers and footers for the most part remained the same and consistent. This was true for use on a computer and on a phone. While the view is a little different, because of size between a phone and computer, the repetitoin is consistent. It makes it very easy for a user to navigate through their website while on various pages within.
